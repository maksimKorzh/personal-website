I prioritizes minimal tech stack

As a full-stack web developer, I have wholeheartedly embraced a minimalist tech stack that prioritizes simplicity, efficiency, and control over the often overwhelming array of options available. Central to my approach is Python with Flask, a lightweight web framework that eschews unnecessary complexity, allowing me to build web applications swiftly and maintainably. Complementing this, I rely on Bootstrap 5 for front-end development, appreciating its responsive design and ease of use. Embracing a philosophy of avoiding unnecessary entanglements with programming languages and frameworks, I leverage jQuery for its concise syntax and versatility in enhancing user interactions. When speed and efficiency are paramount, I turn to plain Golang, particularly for developing fast Terminal User Interface (TUI) applications. This approach, characterized by a refusal to be bound by the dictates of programming languages and frameworks, represents a conscious decision to wield technology rather than being wielded by it. By adhering to a minimalist tech stack, I prioritize clarity, maintainability, and a focus on the essentials, ensuring that my development process remains a mindful and intentional pursuit.
